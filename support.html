Frequently Asked Questions

Why does VToggler need Do Not Disturb access?

Android requires the Do Not Disturb access permission to allow apps to programmatically change your device's ringer mode to Silent.

How do I add a custom image to a widget?

You can customize your widgets directly within the VToggler app settings or by adding the widget to your home screen and tapping the edit button.

Will my settings sync across multiple devices?

No. VToggler respects your privacy and stores all data locally. It does not use cloud storage or require an account.

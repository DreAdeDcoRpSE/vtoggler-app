About VToggler

VToggler is an Android application designed to give you quick and easy control over your device's volume and ringer modes.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a lightweight, privacy-focused tool that helps you manage your device's sound settings without unnecessary bloat, tracking, or advertisements.

Features

  • Quick Toggles: Easily switch between Normal, Vibrate, and Silent modes.
  • Customizable Widgets: Add home screen widgets with your own custom images to control your sound settings instantly.
  • Privacy First: No accounts, no tracking, and no cloud storage. Your data stays on your device.
  • Boot Support: Widgets automatically restore their functionality after your device restarts.

Developer

VToggler is developed by Corpse Visions. We believe in building useful tools that respect user privacy and run efficiently in the background.
